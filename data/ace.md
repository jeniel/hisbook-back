## Acebook Ticketing System Overview

The **Acebook Ticketing System** is an internal platform used at **Allied Care Experts (ACE)
Medical Center – Bohol** to log and manage various technical and administrative concerns.
All employees are required to submit tickets for any **IT-related issues** , **HR matters** ,
**engineering or maintenance requests** , **biomedical equipment concerns** , or other
**operational fixes** to ensure proper documentation, accountability, and timely resolution.

## Missed Logged Procedure

If an employee fails to record a **time in** or **time out** in the attendance system, they must file a
**Missed Logout Ticket** through the Acebook Ticketing System and submit it into the MIS
department for CCTV Review.

## Office Hours Policy

Regular office hours are **8:00 AM to 5:00 PM** , Monday through Friday.
Employees must ensure their attendance logs accurately reflect this schedule unless **prior
approval for flexible working hours** has been granted by their supervisor or the HR
department.
